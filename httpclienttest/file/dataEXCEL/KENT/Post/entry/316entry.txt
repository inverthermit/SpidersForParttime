Entry requirements A good first degree (good second class honours or equivalent) in Business, Marketing, Management, or  a  sciences or social sciences subject, plus  evidence of a good quantitative background (eg a pre-University school qualification in Maths). This programme is not intended for students with a  single or joint economics degree.    General entry requirements Please also see our   general entry requirements .   English language entry requirements For detailed information see our   English language requirements   web pages.
