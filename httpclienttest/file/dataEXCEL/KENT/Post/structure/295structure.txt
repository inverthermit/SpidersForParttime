Course structure


 The following modules are indicative of those offered on this programme. This list is based on the current curriculum and may change year to year in response to new curriculum developments and innovation.

You take  three compulsory modules:

Fundamentals of Philanthropy  - a comprehensive overview of the historic development and current state of philanthropy, drawing on current academic theory, knowledge and empirical research.

Global philanthropy:  comparative perspectives  - exploring philanthropy in Western and non-Western societies, to investigate the universality and specificity of philanthropic traditions and practices.

Understanding Social Research  -  an introduction to the logic and methods of social research, to equip students with rigorous and practical qualitative and quantitative research skills.
