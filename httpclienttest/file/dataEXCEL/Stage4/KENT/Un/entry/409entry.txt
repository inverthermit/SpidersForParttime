Access to HE Diploma    The University of Kent will not necessarily make conditional offers to all access candidates but will continue to assess them on an individual basis. If an offer is made candidates will be required to obtain/pass the overall Access to Higher Education Diploma and may  also be required to obtain a proportion of the total level 3 credits and/or credits in particular subjects at merit grade or above.
