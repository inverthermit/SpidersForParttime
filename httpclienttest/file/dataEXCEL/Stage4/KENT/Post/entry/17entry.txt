Entry requirements A good first degree (2.1 or equivalent) preferably in a quantitative subject  eg  engineering, logistics, mathematics, economics or statistics. Those who do not hold  a degree, but have logistics or supply chain experience,  are also encouraged to apply and will be considered on an individual basis.
