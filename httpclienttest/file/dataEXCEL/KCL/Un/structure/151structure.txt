The Department offers three related undergraduate programmes: the BA in Politics, the BA/BSc in Political Economy and the BA Philosophy, Politics   Economics. The modules students take all degrees will be assessed through a range of techniques including examinations, coursework and tutorial participation.
   
  
     
    YEAR 1
  In Year 1, students take five compulsory modules that give a grounding in the study of political economy.

    YEAR 2
   After Year 1, students are able to choose from two different pathways through the final two years of the degree, depending on their interest.
Students reading for the BA will focus on political analysis and processes, while retaining a core of economics in their courseload. BSc students will pursue a programme weighted more towards economic analysis, with the aim of preparing them for postgraduate study in economics.
