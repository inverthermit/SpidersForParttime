Students study on a full-time basis for 12 months. A range of teaching and learning strategies are used including classroom teaching, web based materials and one-to-one supervision.   
  
     
    YEAR 1
  You will study the following modules: Evidence Based Practice, Research Methods, Dissertation in Healthcare, and 45 credits of optional modules to be agreed with the programme leader. 
     
   
  YEAR 1 CORE
   Research Methods 
 Evidence Based Practice 
 Dissertation in Healthcare   
    
  
    
   
  YEAR 1 OPTIONS
   Students take up to three optional modules of their choosing. Advice on selection is given by their programme leader on an individual basis.
