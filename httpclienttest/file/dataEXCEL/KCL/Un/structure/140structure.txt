    YEAR 2
   Students choose their preferred pathway:
Bipartite: Philosophy and Politics or Philosophy and Economics or Politics and Economics (Students take a total of 60 credits in each of their two chosen areas. Each module is worth 15 credits)
Tripartite: Philosophy, Politics and Economics
(Students take a total of 30 credits in each of their three chosen areas)
