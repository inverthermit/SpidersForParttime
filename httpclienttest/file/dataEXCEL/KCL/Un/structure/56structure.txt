Currently, students study the following core modules; if there are optional modules available the current choices are also shown. King's reviews its optional modules on a regular basis, in order to continue to offer innovative and exciting programmes and this list is therefore indicitive only. Please check here for updates or contact the Faculty/Department for further advice. All students take a selection of compulsory modules and additionally take modules in  Spanish throughout the degree. The degree is split 50/50 beteween European and International Studies modules and Spanish modules.
