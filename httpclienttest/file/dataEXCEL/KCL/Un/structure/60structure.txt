King's reviews its optional modules on a regular basis, in order to continue to offer innovative and exciting programmes and this list is therefore subject to change. Please check here for updates, or contact the Department(s) for further advice.

   YEAR 3
   This is normally spent abroad in French- and German-speaking  countries.
Assessment for this year will depend on whether you choose to split the year  between two countries, or spend the entire year in one. It will also depend how  you choose to spend your year: as an exchange student at a French or German  university, as a language assistant teaching English, or on an approved work  placement (only available  for German).
