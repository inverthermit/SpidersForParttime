The Department offers three related undergraduate programmes: the BA/BSc in Politics, the BA/BSc in Political Economy and the BA (Hons) in Philosophy, Politics   Economics. The modules students take on all degrees will be assessed through a range of techniques including examinations, coursework and tutorial participation.   
  
     
    YEAR 1
  In Year 1, students take 1 core and 4 compulsory modules. Core modules must be passed to progress. 
     
   
  YEAR 1 CORE
   First year modules are all compulsory. Core modules must be passed to progress.
Core:
Studying Politics (15 credits)
Compulsory:
World Politics Since 1945 (30 credits)
Comparing Political Systems (30 credits)
Introduction to Political Theory (30 credits)
Research Methods for Politics (15 credits)
