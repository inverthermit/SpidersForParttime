Currently, students study the following core modules shown below.  Optional modules are available and the  current choices are shown. King's reviews its optional modules on a regular basis, in order to continue to offer innovative and exciting programmes and this list is therefore subject to change. Please check here for updates, or contact the School/department for further advice.
