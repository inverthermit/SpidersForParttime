ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   2:1 undergraduate degree (or equivalent overseas qualification) in history, politics or a relevant discipline, e.g. public policy, law or social science.   Evidence of achievement of an academic level comparable to at least 2:1 honours standard through past studies, and where previous study, work or experience (for example, in government or NGO service) has made the applicant a suitable candidate, will also be considered.
