ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice    Minimum academic requirements:  2:1 BA degree with honours (or overseas equivalent) in Classics, Classical Studies, A or a closely related subject.   Applicants must be able to read texts in Latin and/or Greek.   The Department offers a  Graduate Diploma in Classical Studies , which can act as a conversion course for those with degrees in other areas who want to go on to take the MA Classics.
