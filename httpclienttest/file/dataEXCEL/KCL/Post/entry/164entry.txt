ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice     Normally minimum 2:1 undergraduate degree (or international equivalent)   in a related subject such as Physiology, Biomedical/Biological Sciences, Sports & Exercise Science and Physiotherapy, Psychology, Neuroscience, Medicine. Biomedical Engineering graduate, and possibly engineering and physical science graduates will be considered on a case by case basis.   Relevant experience(s) may also be considered.
