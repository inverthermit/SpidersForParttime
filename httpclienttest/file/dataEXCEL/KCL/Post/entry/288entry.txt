ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice     MPharm or equivalent. First degree taught and examined in English or ILTS Level 7. Current registration with the General Pharmaceutical Council or the Pharmaceutical Society of Northern Ireland. Practising pharmacist in a patient-facing role within community or primary care.  NB additional entry requirements apply to the Practice Certificate in Independent Prescribing &ndash; see website for further detail  http://www.kcl.ac.uk/lsm/study/departments/pharmacy-forensic/CPD/prescribing.aspx
