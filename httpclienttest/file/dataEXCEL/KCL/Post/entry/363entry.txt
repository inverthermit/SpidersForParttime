ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice    Academic requirements:  A 2:1 undergraduate degree (or international equivalent) is usually required  in a relevant subject (for example: a foreign language;  English; Linguistics/Applied Linguistics/Language and Communication; Psychology; a social science subject.)   Alternative qualifications may be considered. Work experience:  No previous experience of language teaching is required (although any experience including informal tutoring or volunteer teaching   and/or observation of teaching) will be taken into account in assessing your application
