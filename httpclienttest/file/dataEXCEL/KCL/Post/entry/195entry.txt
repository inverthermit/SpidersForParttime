ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   1st Class or good 2:1 class   undergraduate degree (65 per cent or above in the UK marking system) required in a social science related area (e.g. management, economics, psychology, history, sociology, law or other appropriate social science subject) or an equivalent overseas qualification.   Please note a science/engineering degree may be considered where social science skills have been an important component of the degree or a candidate has significant marketing practice experience.
