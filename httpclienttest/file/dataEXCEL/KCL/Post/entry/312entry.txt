ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice     Bachelor's degree with 2:1 honours or above (or overseas equivalent) with nowledge and understanding of the subject you intend to teach.   GCSE grade C or above, or an equivalent qualification, in English Language and Mathematics. Applicants without these English or Mathematics qualifications at the time they apply may be considered, but they must obtain them, or pass an equivalent test set by King's, before the course starts.   Applicants for Modern Foreign Languages need to offer two languages, either French and German or French and Spanish, with qualifications equivalent to at least AS-level in the subsidiary language.  For further guidance, see our detailed FAQs   Note: enhanced criminal conviction disclosures (Disclosure & Barring Service) and occupational health checks apply to this course. Please contact the Admissions Office if you have any questions.
