ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice    Minimum academic requirements:  2:1 BA degree with honours (or overseas equivalent) in Classics, Classical Studies, Ancient History, Classical Archaeology, History or a closely related subject. High Desirable:  It is highly desirable for candidates to have had experience of learning one or more relevant ancient languages. This may be as part of school or undergraduate education, or through a summer school. The department runs a range of summer language courses:  www.kcl.ac.uk/artshums/depts/classics/study/summerclass/index.aspx   www.kcl.ac.uk/artshums/depts/classics/study/intensive.aspx The Department also offers a  Graduate Diploma in Classical Studies , which can act as a conversion course for those with degrees in other areas who want to go on to take the MA Ancient History.
