ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Applicants should hold a relevant first degree (minimum 2:1) or an equivalent professional qualification. Experience in the workplace is desirable but not essential.  It is important that applicants for the Organisational Psychiatry & Psychology MSc have undertaken some Psychology modules in their undergraduate qualification - a solid foundation in this Psychology is essential for the demands of this programme.
