ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Bachelors degree with 2:1 honours (or overseas equivalent e.g. USA CGPA 3.3) in history, international relations, political science, economics or other appropriate subject.  Successful applicants will generally be achieving grades at mid 2:1 level (or equivalent overseas level) or higher.   Candidates who do not achieve a 2:1 but have professional or voluntary experience will also be considered. Mature candidates will be considered favourably.
