ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Bachelors degree with 2:1 honours degree (or international equivalent) in in computer science, mathematics, physics, chemistry, electrical engineering, or a joint degree in two such subjects.  Applicants should also demonstrate a competence in a high level programming language such as Pascal, C, C++, Java, etc, to the level expected at the end of the first year of a BSc honours degree in computer science  We may lower entry qualifications for students with substantial relevant work experience.
