ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Bachelor's degree with first class honours (or international equivalent) in any relevant discipline including mathematics, physics, chemistry, engineering, materials science, biophysics, geophysical sciences and computer science.   Note: A 2:1 (upper second class) honours degree (or international equivalent) may be acceptable depending on the candidate's academic background.   A strong background in quantitative science will be essential for students to take full advantage of the programme.
