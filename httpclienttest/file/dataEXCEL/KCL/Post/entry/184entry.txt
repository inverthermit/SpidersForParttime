ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Bachelors degree with 2:1 honours degree (or international equivalent) in computer science/IT.   We may lower entry qualifications for students with substantial relevant work experience. Note  that English language requirements for this programme are higher than other MA/MSc programmes within the Faculty of Natural & Mathematical Sciences. IELTS 7.0 overall (with no lower than 6.0 in any one skill) is required (or an equivalent acceptable proficiency test).
