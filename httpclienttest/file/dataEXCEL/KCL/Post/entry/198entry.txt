ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice    Academic Requirements   You must have graduated from university with a good GPA (equivalent to a UK minimum upper second class degree, or "2:1") and have completed a full undergraduate degree programme. It is your responsibility to ensure that your GPA is sufficient for the postgraduate programmes you wish to apply for. English Language requirements   There are September and January entry points to this programme.  The minimum language requirement for students joining in  September  is IELTS 5.5 (with a minimum of 5.5 in reading and writing and 5.0 in listening and speaking)  For students joining in  January , the minimum language requirement is IELTS 6.0 (with a minimum of 5.5 in all skills)  If you are considering taking a different English language proficiency test, check for acceptable tests and levels on our  website here.
