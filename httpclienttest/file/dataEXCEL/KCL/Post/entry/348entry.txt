ACADEMIC ENTRY REQUIREMENTS     General entry advice   Bachelor's degree with 2:1 honours (or overseas equivalent) in history, international relations, political science, economics or other appropriate subject.  As a guideline for those with USA undergraduate qualifications, we are usually looking for a CGPA of above  3.3. Applicants with qualifications from other countries are welcomed, with further guidance available at  www.kcl.ac.uk/study/international/yourcountry/index.aspx
