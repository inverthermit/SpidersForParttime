   FORMAT AND ASSESSMENT
  Modular programme consisting of 360 credits, plus for UK specialist training an additional 180 credits of non-modular supervised clinical work (540 credits in total). Modules are grouped into three distinct types: 
Three taught modules, covering the scientific and clinical basis of Periodontology, assessed by written examination;
Six clinical modules, involving supervised clinical practice and examined by a mixture of case studies on treated and unseen patients, practical, or oral examinations;
One research module, involving the submission of a research project report including a literature review, with an oral examination.
