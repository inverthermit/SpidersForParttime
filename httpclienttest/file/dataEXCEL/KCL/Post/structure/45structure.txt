STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
     
  Core programme content
   Stem Cells in Cellular Therapies and Regenerative Medicine (45 credits)
Cellular Therapies in Immunology (15 credits)
Commercialisation of Cellular Therapy (45 credits)
Dissertation on business strategy development for cellular therapy (60 credits)

     
   Indicative non-core content
   Clinical Drug Development (15 credits)
Ethics of Clinical Research (15 credits)

     
   
   FORMAT AND ASSESSMENT
  
Students are mainly assessed by
coursework, reports, class tests, poster,
oral presentations and development of a
credible business plan. There is a
formal exam for one optional module
